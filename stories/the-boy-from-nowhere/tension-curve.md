# Tension Curve: The Boy From Nowhere

## Tension Scale
- **0.0-0.2**: Calm, reflective, resolution
- **0.2-0.4**: Low tension, character development, warmth
- **0.4-0.6**: Moderate tension, complications, emotional stakes
- **0.6-0.8**: High tension, conflict, crisis points
- **0.8-1.0**: Peak tension, climax, breaking points

## Rule: No more than 3 consecutive high-intensity chapters without a breather.

## Tension Shape (Final — with Interludes)

The interludes function as deliberate tension valleys. They create a breathing rhythm that prevents reader fatigue and makes the peaks land harder.


**Key**: × = chapter, i = interlude. Numbers indicate chapter/interlude labels.

**Rhythm pattern**: The interludes (marked i) create valleys at 0.15-0.40 that let the reader breathe before the next ascent. The deepest valley is Interlude G (0.15 — pure comedy during COVID lockdown), placed just before the India captaincy climax (0.70).

**Climactic peaks**: Ch 10 (0.75, auction), Ch 28 (0.75, injury), Ch 33 (0.85, dark night), Ch 36 (0.90, trophy).

**The descent**: Ch 37-42 trends downward from 0.40 to 0.20, with one final spike at Ch 39 (0.70, captaincy innings). The story doesn't end with a bang — it ends with a man sitting on temple steps, teaching his son about a pendant. Tension: 0.20. Earned peace.
