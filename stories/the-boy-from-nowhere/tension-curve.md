# Tension Curve: The Boy From Nowhere

## Tension Scale
- **0.0-0.2**: Calm, reflective, worldbuilding
- **0.2-0.4**: Low tension, character development, setup
- **0.4-0.6**: Moderate tension, complications, emotional stakes
- **0.6-0.8**: High tension, conflict, crisis points
- **0.8-1.0**: Peak tension, climax, breaking points

## Rule: No more than 3 consecutive high-intensity chapters without a breather.
